# Template: Scientific (domestic) consultant review

> **Purpose:** the scientific consultant's review of the candidate's work and the dissertation;
> published online and read out at the defense (see [[../01-defense-process/session-procedure]], item 5).
> Prepared in free form, but follows the structure below.

## Header

```
REVIEW
of the scientific consultant on the dissertation of <candidate full name>
on the topic "<...>", submitted for the degree of doctor of philosophy (PhD)
Educational programme: <8D061XX — name>
```

## Content structure

1. **General characteristics of the candidate and topic** — relevance and timeliness of the research.
2. **The candidate's work during the studies** — independence, scientific maturity, ability to
   formulate and solve problems.
3. **Scientific novelty and contribution** — what new results were obtained.
4. **Reliability and validity of the results** — methods, experiments, approbation.
5. **Publication activity** — compliance with the requirements (WoS/Scopus, KKSON, conferences;
   see [[../00-regulatory-framework/publication-requirements]]).
6. **Practical significance and implementation.**
7. **Conclusion:** the dissertation meets the requirements for works for the PhD degree;
   the candidate deserves the award of the doctor of philosophy (PhD) degree under programme <...>.

## Signatory details

```
Scientific consultant:
   Full name: ...
   Academic degree / title: ...
   Position, organization: ...
   Signature: ___________  Date: "__" ______ 20__
```
