# Template: Foreign scientific consultant review

> **Purpose:** the review by a foreign scientific consultant. It has a **fixed structure of 5
> numbered sections** + a "Reviewer information" block. Often prepared in Russian/English.
> Read out at the defense (see [[../01-defense-process/session-procedure]], item 5).

## Header

```
REVIEW
of the foreign scientific consultant on the dissertation of <candidate full name>
on the topic "<...>", submitted for the degree of doctor of philosophy (PhD)
Educational programme: <8D061XX — name>
```

## Content structure (5 sections)

1. **General overview** — relevance, timeliness, socio-economic significance of the topic;
   justification of the need for the proposed approach.
2. **Scientific novelty and methodological contribution** — the key scientific contribution, the
   proposed models/architectures/mechanisms, how they differ from existing approaches.
3. **Experimental evaluation and results** — datasets, data volume, metrics (e.g. mAP, mIoU,
   accuracy, speed), robustness, comparison with baseline models.
4. **Practical significance** — scalability, cost-effectiveness, applicability in real-world
   scenarios, implementation potential.
5. **Conclusion** — the final assessment: the work makes a significant, original and scientifically
   substantiated contribution; meets the academic and methodological requirements for doctoral
   research and the international standards for awarding a PhD; the author deserves the degree.
