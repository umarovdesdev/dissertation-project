# PhD defense lifecycle (from pre-defense to registration)

> The end-to-end route of a defense before the 8D061 dissertation council at IITU («МУИТ»).
> Shows **which document appears at which stage** and where its template lives.

## Stages

### 1. Preliminary review (pre-defense)
- Extended meeting of the relevant department/council; **2 reviewers** appointed.
- Outcome: a **conclusion** recommending the dissertation for defense (§5.4 of the Statute).
- Stage documents: [[../17-ethics-committee-conclusion/structure|ethics committee conclusion]],
  scientific consultants' reviews.
- Details: [[preliminary-review]].

### 2. Admission of the dissertation to defense
- The candidate submits the document set to the academic secretary (§4.2 of the Statute).
- The council **admits the dissertation to defense** at a session.
- Prepared: [[../19-doctoral-candidate-info/structure|candidate information]],
  [[../18-list-of-publications/structure|list of scientific papers]].

### 3. Publication of materials online
- A set time before the defense, the council posts 15 materials on the website
  (announcement, dissertation, annotation, reviews, video recording, etc.).
- Prepared: [[../12-defense-announcement/structure|defense announcement]],
  [[../11-abstract-annotation/structure|abstract/annotation (3 languages)]].
- Details: [[materials-published-online]].

### 4. Reviews
- [[../13-supervisor-review/structure|Scientific (domestic) consultant review]].
- [[../14-foreign-consultant-review/structure|Foreign scientific consultant review]].
- [[../15-official-reviewer-report/structure|Written reports of official reviewers]] (form Appendix 3).
- [[../16-review/structure|Reviews]] and informal feedback.

### 5. Session (defense)
- In-person session per the §7.7 agenda; the [[../20-attendance-sheet/structure|attendance sheet]] is kept.
- Secret ballot (≥3/4).
- May be held remotely — see [[online-session]].
- Details: [[session-procedure]], [[voting-and-appeal]].

### 6. Formalizing results and registration
- Council decision; if needed — appeal (§8).
- State registration of the PhD dissertation (Rules No. 472 of 01.10.2024).

### 7. Annual council reporting
- [[../21-council-annual-report/structure|Report on the work of the dissertation council]] (Appendix 1) — yearly.
