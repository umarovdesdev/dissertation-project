# Preliminary review (pre-defense)

> Section 5 of the Statute on the council. The stage preceding admission of the dissertation to defense.

## Procedure (§5)

1. The dissertation is considered at an **extended meeting** of the relevant department
   (structural unit) with the participation of subject-matter specialists.
2. **2 reviewers** are appointed for the preliminary review.
3. A **conclusion** is issued on the possibility (recommendation) of submitting the dissertation
   for defense.

## Content of the preliminary review conclusion (§5.4)

The conclusion typically reflects:

- relevance of the topic and its alignment with directions of science development / state programmes;
- scientific novelty and reliability of the results;
- compliance with the principles of independence and internal unity;
- completeness of presenting the main results in publications (see [[../00-regulatory-framework/publication-requirements]]);
- compliance of the formatting with the requirements (see [[../02-formatting/gost-formatting]]);
- a recommendation for defense under a specific educational programme (8D06101/8D06102/8D06103/8D06105).
