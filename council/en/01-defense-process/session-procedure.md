# Dissertation council session procedure (defense)

> Procedure of the in-person defense session. Section 7 of the Statute (§7.7 — defense agenda).
> This is the reference scenario for a defense in 8D061 at IITU («МУИТ»).

## Session agenda (§7.7)

1. **Chairman's opening remarks** — opening the session, checking the quorum.
2. **The academic secretary** reports on the submitted documents and their compliance,
   reads out the [[../19-doctoral-candidate-info/structure|candidate information]].
3. **Candidate's presentation** — up to **20 minutes** (relevance, aim, objectives, methods,
   results, novelty, statements for defense, practical significance).
4. **Questions** from council members and attendees, candidate's answers.
5. **Statements by scientific consultants** (domestic and foreign) — see
   [[../13-supervisor-review/structure]], [[../14-foreign-consultant-review/structure]].
6. **Statements by official reviewers** — reading the [[../15-official-reviewer-report/structure|written reports]].
7. **Candidate's responses** to the reviewers' comments.
8. **Informal feedback** (received on the abstract/annotation), see [[../16-review/structure]].
9. **Scientific discussion** — statements by council members and attendees.
10. **Candidate's closing remarks.**
11. **Secret ballot** of council members (≥3/4 — see [[voting-and-appeal]]).
12. **Announcement of results**, drafting the council's conclusion.

## Attendance record

During the session the [[../20-attendance-sheet/structure|attendance sheet of council members]]
(Appendix 4) is kept — confirmation of the quorum.
