# Materials published online before the defense

> Section 6 of the Statute (§6.9). Before the defense, the council posts a set of materials in open
> access on the official website. Knowing this list determines which documents need to be prepared
> and published.

## List of published materials (§6.9, ~15 items)

1. **Defense announcement / notice** — see [[../12-defense-announcement/structure]].
2. **Dissertation** (full text) — see [[../10-dissertation/structure]].
3. **Annotation (abstract)** in **three languages** (Kazakh, Russian, English), up to
   **15 pages**, describing the topic, aim, objectives, methods, main statements, novelty,
   significance — see [[../11-abstract-annotation/structure]].
4. **List of the candidate's publications** — see [[../18-list-of-publications/structure]].
5. **Scientific consultants' reviews** (domestic and foreign) —
   [[../13-supervisor-review/structure]], [[../14-foreign-consultant-review/structure]].
6. **Official reviewers' reports** — [[../15-official-reviewer-report/structure]].
7. **Video recording** of the session.
8. ... and other materials envisaged by §6.9 (conclusions, consents, certificates).

## Requirements for official reviewers (§6)

- They are leading scientists in the relevant specialties/educational programmes.
- They have publications in international scientific journals.
- §6.9 regulates the set and the deadlines for publishing materials.
