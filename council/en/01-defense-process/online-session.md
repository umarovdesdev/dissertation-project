# Online council session (videoconference)

> "Methodological instructions for organizing the dissertation council session online in the form
> of a videoconference", approved by the Vice-Rector for Research of IITU («МУИТ») (developed in
> accordance with Model Statute No. 126 of 31.03.2011 and the
> [[../00-regulatory-framework/council-statute|Statute on the council]]).
> Applies when the defense is held remotely. The agenda is the same as in person
> (see [[session-procedure]]) — only the organization and voting differ.

## Chapter 1. General provisions

Goal — a uniform procedure for holding the dissertation defense session by videoconference.

## Chapter 2. Organization of the defense (requirements)

- Notification of the candidate, council members and official reviewers — **at least 5 working days**
  before the defense (email + posting on the council's website).
- Provided for: **visual identification** of all participants; uninterrupted video and audio
  streaming; **recording** of the session; **anonymous voting**.
- Platform — one of those available (**Zoom, Microsoft Teams**, etc.).
- Each participant needs: the application, a device with a **webcam**, speakers/headphones, a microphone.
  Use of mobile phones is **not recommended**.
- On connecting, participants state their **full name** and turn on the **camera** for identification.
- Before the session the academic secretary verifies the **identity** of the candidate and council members.
- Before the defense the secretary and the candidate check the presence of the presentation, electronic
  versions of reviews, the draft conclusion and other documents to be shown.
- **Video recording** is done with the platform's built-in feature (secretary together with the candidate).
- During statements, the other participants **mute their microphones**; to ask a question — "raise hand".

## Chapter 3. Secret ballot (via Google Forms)

- The **Google Forms** service is used for the secret ballot.
- Voting rights are held by council members and official reviewers; in advance they:
  - create a **Gmail** account;
  - sign in to Gmail in one of the browsers (**Mozilla, Chrome, Opera**) and keep it open;
  - provide their Gmail addresses to the academic secretary **before** the defense.
- The academic secretary prepares **a separate Google Form for each candidate** in advance.
- After the chairman announces the start of voting, the secretary sends the form link to the
  voters' Gmail addresses.
- On completion, the secretary **displays the results on screen** and announces them.
- Voting thresholds are the same (≥3/4), see [[voting-and-appeal]].
