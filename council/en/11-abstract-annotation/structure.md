# Template: Abstract / Annotation (trilingual)

> **Purpose:** a concise account of the dissertation, published online before the defense and
> circulated. At the IITU council it is prepared **in three languages** (Kazakh, Russian, English),
> up to **15 pages** per language (§6.9 of the Statute).
> **This is the most frequently requested document — follow this structure, not generic templates.**

## Mandatory requirements

- Languages: **Kazakh / Russian / English** — three separate versions, identical in structure.
- Length — up to 15 pages in each language.
- Contains: topic, aim, objectives, methods, main statements for defense, novelty, significance.
- Formatting — per [[../02-formatting/gost-formatting|GOST]] (title + reverse for the abstract booklet).

## Structure (order of the "General characteristics of the work" sections)

1. **Relevance of the research topic** — state of the problem, justification of need.
2. **Aim of the research** — a single statement.
3. **Objectives of the research** — a numbered list (usually mapped to chapters).
4. **Object of the research.**
5. **Subject of the research.**
6. **Methodology and methods of the research.**
7. **Scientific novelty** — a numbered list of new results.
8. **Statements submitted for defense** — a numbered list (each defended statement).
9. **Theoretical significance.**
10. **Practical significance** (including implementation, implementation acts).
11. **Reliability of the results** — how it is ensured (data volume, metrics, comparison).
12. **Empirical / experimental basis** — datasets, data volume.
13. **Approbation of the results** — conferences, seminars; **connection with scientific programmes**
    (subpara. 2 para. 3 art. 20 of the Law of the RK "On Science…", state programmes).
14. **Publications** — referencing the [[../18-list-of-publications/structure|list of papers]]
    (WoS/Scopus with quartile and percentile; KKSON; conferences; copyright certificates).
15. **Structure and length** of the dissertation.
16. **Main content of the work** — a brief account by chapter.
17. **Conclusion** — main findings.
18. **List of published works on the dissertation topic.**
