# Template: Review / informal feedback

> **Purpose:** a review of the dissertation/abstract or informal feedback received on the published
> annotation. Free form; informal feedback is read out at the defense
> (see [[../01-defense-process/session-procedure]], item 8).
> To be distinguished from the strictly formalized
> [[../15-official-reviewer-report/structure|official reviewer report]].

## Header

```
REVIEW (FEEDBACK)
on the dissertation (abstract) of <candidate full name>
on the topic "<...>", submitted for the degree of doctor of philosophy (PhD)
Educational programme: <8D061XX>
```

## Content structure

1. **Relevance of the topic.**
2. **Brief content of the work** — structure, main results.
3. **Scientific novelty and reliability.**
4. **Practical significance.**
5. **Comments** (if any).
6. **Conclusion** — compliance with the requirements for PhD works; recommendation.

## Signatory details

```
   Full name: ...
   Academic degree / title, position, organization: ...
   Signature: ___________  Date: "__" ______ 20__
```
