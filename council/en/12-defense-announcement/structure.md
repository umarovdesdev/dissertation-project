# Template: Defense announcement / notice

> **Purpose:** the official notice of the upcoming defense, published on the council's website
> (§6.9.1 of the Statute). Usually prepared in three languages (KZ/RU/EN).
> **Signed by:** the academic secretary of the dissertation council.

## Contents of the notice (§6.9.1)

1. **Header:** name of the organization (IITU / «МУИТ»), the dissertation council in the field
   **8D061 — Information and Communication Technologies**.
2. **Candidate information:** full name, educational programme (8D06101 / 8D06102 / 8D06103 / 8D06105),
   dissertation topic, sought degree — doctor of philosophy (PhD).
3. **Scientific consultants:** domestic and foreign (full name, degree/title, organization, country).
4. **Official reviewers:** full name, degree/title, organization.
5. **Temporary council members** engaged for this defense (see [[../00-regulatory-framework/council-composition]]).
6. **Form of submission** of the dissertation (in person), **date, time and place** of the defense, address.
7. **Where to access** the dissertation and annotation (library / website), date of circulating the abstract.
