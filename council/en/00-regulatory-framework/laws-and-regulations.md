# Legal and regulatory framework for the PhD defense

> List of the regulatory acts that govern the defense of a PhD dissertation before the
> dissertation council of IITU («МУИТ») in the field
> **8D061 — Information and Communication Technologies**.
> Used as the reference base in all documents (reviews, conclusions, the announcement).

## Laws of the Republic of Kazakhstan

| Act | Details | Scope |
|-----|---------|-------|
| Law of the RK "On Science" (now "On Science and Technology Policy") | No. 407-IV of 18.02.2011 | Award of academic degrees, science priorities (subpara. 2 para. 3 art. 20 — directions of science development set by the Supreme Scientific and Technical Commission, VNTK, under the Government of the RK) |
| Law of the RK "On Education" | No. 319-III of 27.07.2007 | Postgraduate education, PhD doctoral studies |

> In reviews and reports, alignment of the topic with the "directions of science development"
> is justified by referring to **subpara. 2) para. 3 art. 20 of the Law of the RK "On Science
> and Technology Policy"** and/or to state programmes (e.g. "Digital Kazakhstan",
> Government Decree of the RK No. 827 of 12.12.2017).

## Formatting standards

Application details — see [[../02-formatting/gost-formatting]].

## Supervisory bodies

- **KKSON MSHE RK** (Committee for Quality Assurance in Science and Higher Education) — maintains
  the List of recommended journals (see [[kkson-journal-list]]).
- **VNTK under the Government of the RK** (Supreme Scientific and Technical Commission) — sets
  the priority directions of science development.
- **NCSTE** (National Center for State Scientific and Technical Expertise) — plagiarism/borrowings check.
- **NIIS / NIIP of the Ministry of Justice RK** — registration of copyright certificates and databases.
