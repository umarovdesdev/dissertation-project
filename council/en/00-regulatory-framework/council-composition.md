# Composition and organization of the dissertation council

> Structure of the council in the field **8D061 — Information and Communication Technologies**
> at IITU («МУИТ»). Describes **roles and requirements**, with no reference to specific individuals.
> The council admits dissertations for the following educational programmes:
> 8D06101 — Intelligent Systems; 8D06102 — Computer and Software Engineering;
> 6D070300/8D06103 — Information Systems; 8D06105 — Data Science.

## Composition (roles)

| Role | Typical qualification requirements |
|------|-------------------------------------|
| Council chairman | Doctor of Sciences (D.Sc., e.g. Eng.), professor |
| Deputy chairman | PhD / Doctor of Sciences |
| Academic secretary | Candidate of Sciences (Cand.Sc.), associate professor |
| Council member | PhD / Doctor of Sciences, associate professor |

## Formation rules (from the Statute, §2)

- Council term — **3 years**.
- Size — **no more than 7 members**.
- **At least 3** members from other universities/research organizations.
- **4 permanent** members (chairman, deputy, academic secretary + 1) and **up to 3 temporary**
  (engaged for a specific defense by the topic's profile).
- **≥4 members** with a publication threshold: **≥3 articles** in Q1–Q3 (JCR/Web of Science)
  or with a CiteScore percentile (Scopus) **≥35**.

## Permanent / temporary members

- **Permanent** members serve for the entire term of the council.
- **Temporary** members are added by order for a specific dissertation defense to provide
  subject-matter expertise on the topic (their names appear in the
  [[../12-defense-announcement/structure|defense announcement]] and in the
  [[../20-attendance-sheet/structure|attendance sheet]]).

## Changing the composition

The composition of permanent members is approved and amended by **order** (e.g. "On amendments
to the composition of permanent members of the dissertation council of IITU", bilingual KZ/RU,
signed by the Acting Chairman of the Board — Rector). The order states the roles, academic
degrees/titles, the field 8D061 and the term of office.

## Signatories of the council's official documents

| Document | Signed by |
|----------|-----------|
| Council annual report | Chairman + Academic secretary + seal |
| Attendance sheet | Academic secretary |
| Candidate information | Academic secretary + seal |
| Defense announcement | Academic secretary |
| List of scientific papers | Candidate + Academic secretary of the Academic Council |
