> Reference to the official List of journals recommended by the Committee for Quality Assurance
> in Science and Higher Education (KKSON) of the MSHE RK for publishing the main results of
> dissertations. Used for category 2 in the
> [[../18-list-of-publications/structure|list of scientific papers]].

## How to use

1. An article on the PhD topic (8D061) is published either in **WoS/Scopus** (category 1) or in a
   journal from this **KKSON List** (category 2) — see [[publication-requirements]].
2. In the list of papers, for each article state the journal, year, issue, pages, DOI;
   for WoS/Scopus — the quartile and percentile.
