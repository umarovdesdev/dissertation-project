# Template: List of scientific papers

> **Purpose:** the list of the candidate's publications on the dissertation topic; published online.
> **Signed by:** the Candidate + the Academic secretary of the Academic Council.
> Composition requirements — see [[../00-regulatory-framework/publication-requirements]].

## Header

```
LIST OF SCIENTIFIC PAPERS
of the PhD candidate under educational programme <8D061XX — name>, IITU («МУИТ»)
<candidate full name>
```

## Table form (columns)

| No. | Title of the paper | Type of work (print/electronic) | Name of the publisher, journal (No., year), copyright certificate No. | Number of printer's sheets | Names of co-authors |
|-----|--------------------|---------------------------------|-----------------------------------------------------------------------|----------------------------|---------------------|

## Grouping (mandatory order of sections)

The table is divided into grouped sections — in exactly this order:

1. **Publications in journals indexed in the international information resources
   Web of Science (Clarivate Analytics) and Scopus.**
   For each article also state: **DOI**, **quartile** (Q1–Q4), **CiteScore percentile**.
2. **Publications in journals recommended by KKSON MSHE RK** (see [[../00-regulatory-framework/kkson-journal-list]]).
3. **Publications in proceedings of international scientific-practical conferences.**
4. **Copyright certificate / certificate of state registration** (computer program, database):
   number and date, registering body (NIIS/NIIP of the Ministry of Justice RK).
