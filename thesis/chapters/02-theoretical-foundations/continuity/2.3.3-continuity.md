**Key concepts established:**
- **In-domain (ophthalmology-specific) self-supervised pretraining** of the CNN backbone on an unlabelled retinal corpus → retina-aware initialization before any DR label is seen; the integrated arm's pretraining half (vs ImageNet-supervised baseline).
- **SSL principle and design space** (from #92, #73): solve a label-free pretext objective on unlabelled data, then fine-tune. Families: **contrastive** (SimCLR #86 NT-Xent; MoCo #87 momentum encoder + queue), **negative-free** (BYOL #88; SimSiam #91 stop-gradient anti-collapse), **self-distillation** (DINO #89), **masked generative** (MAE #90, 75% masking). Reported on natural images (ImageNet) → design space, not retinal performance (SIR-3).
- **In-domain > natural-image evidence:** MICLe (Azizi #85, non-retinal: +6.7% derm top-1, +1.1% CXR mAUC over ImageNet baseline — extrapolation caveat) and **RETFound (Zhou #84, keystone)**: MAE/ViT on ~1.6M retinal images; the source reported it outperformed SimCLR/DINO/MoCo-v3, largest gains under limited labels. Two independent lines (SIR-5).
- **CNN-vs-ViT nuance (key analytical move):** RETFound/MAE/DINO are ViT; the dissertation's backbones are CNNs (ResNet-50/EfficientNet-B3, §3.2.1). The convolution-native in-domain path rests on the ResNet-compatible contrastive/negative-free family + MICLe; RETFound establishes the *principle*, the CNN realization is chosen empirically.
- **Bounding:** DGL-6 (transfer benefit not guaranteed, empirical only); **CFC-2.8** (composite IV — SSL initialization reported only within the integrated configuration, never independently attributable, never paired with "preprocessing dominates"); no clinical-validation register (CFC-2.4).
- **Inherited limitations (SIR-2):** RETFound retrospective, corpus provenance uncontrolled, **no preprocessing ablation** (cannot adjudicate preprocessing-vs-pretraining); MICLe non-retinal.

**Terms introduced (now available without re-definition):** self-supervised pretraining, pretext objective, in-domain / ophthalmology-specific pretraining, contrastive learning, NT-Xent, momentum encoder, negative-free SSL, stop-gradient, self-distillation, masked autoencoder / masked image modelling, foundation model, label efficiency, multi-instance contrastive (MICLe).
