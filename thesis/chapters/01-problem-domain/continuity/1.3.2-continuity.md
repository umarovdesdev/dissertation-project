**Key concepts established:**
- Initialization/adaptation is a **first-order, co-equal determinant** of a fixed backbone's DR performance (not a footnote); it is the axis along which the integrated arm most fundamentally departs from baseline.
- **ImageNet transfer is the field default** (Gulshan #12 Inception-v3; Saxena #02 InceptionResNetV2, AUC 0.958/0.92 Messidor-1/2 binary; Xu #09 EfficientNet+Swin, 0.97 APTOS 5-class) — cited as prevailing *practice*, not compared (SIR-3).
- **Domain gap**: ImageNet natural images ≠ fundus tissue; transfer is an in-practice success, not guaranteed (DGL-6).
- Label scarcity motivates semi-/self-supervised learning (Cheplygina #73, *general* survey — framing only, no retinal result; SIR-1/2).
- **In-domain SSL** = learning retinal representations from unlabeled fundus images (no DR labels). **Explicit THIN/GAP flag:** no retinal-SSL primary sources (SimCLR/MoCo/BYOL/DINO-on-fundus) in corpus; integrated-arm SSL is the **candidate's own methodological design** (→ §3.3.2), not a literature result.
