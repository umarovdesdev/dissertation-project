- **Three cumulative critical conclusions (the gap):**
  1. **Non-comparability (SIR-3):** endpoints (binary rDR / 4-class ICDR / VTDR), datasets, populations, devices, reference standards all differ → no ranking possible; field lacks a common-protocol basis.
  2. **Reproducibility fragility:** public-data reproduction degrades sharply (Voets 0.990→0.853, §1.2.2); systematic review (#39 Senapati) flags overfitting, class imbalance, weak early-stage detection.
  3. **Preprocessing treated as ancillary & under-reported (P1 practice, SIR-9):** Gulshan defers to supplement; Zhang/Ruamviboonsuk don't report it; cross-dataset CNNs mention normalization only in passing → preprocessing's contribution rarely isolated/formalized/evaluated. **This is the analytical bridge to the central thesis (P2: preprocessing as integral model component).**
- Deployment landscape (#22, SELF) is narrow/opaque/binary (IDx-DR DR-only; DeepMind needs advanced equipment; Orbis connectivity-dependent); projected KZ outcomes third-party (SIR-8); off-modality #43 OCTA.
- **The gap is methodological, not a performance deficit:** formalize + fully report preprocessing as integral, evaluate under controlled, multi-dataset, transparent conditions (cross-dataset transfer + device shift). **No superiority claim over any named system (CFC-2.2/NC-2); no head-to-head comparison.**
