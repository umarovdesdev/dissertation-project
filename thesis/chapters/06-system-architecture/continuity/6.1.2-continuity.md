**Key concepts established:** the module decomposition (Table 6.3) of the screening system into seven loosely-coupled, independently-replaceable modules — Ingestion, Preprocessing Engine, Inference, Decision-Support/Reporting, Clinician Interface, Data-Management/PACS-EHR Integration, Orchestration/Error-Handling — each mapped to its originating FR(s) and governing NFR(s); the design principle that the OD-6 envelope determines the **topology** (the arrangement), not only the membership, of the module set; the central analytical result that the PACS/EHR integration layer must be **asynchronous (store-and-forward)** so the inference critical path is not coupled to a synchronous hospital-system round trip — a synchronous/cloud-centric topology is inadmissible under NFR-1/NFR-2/NFR-4.
