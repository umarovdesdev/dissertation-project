### CONTENT SPECIFICATION

**Section objective:** Decompose the screening system into a set of independently-replaceable modules, map each module to the functional requirements of Table 6.1, and demonstrate that the chosen modular topology — including the PACS/EHR integration layer — is an admissible realization of the OD-6 non-functional envelope fixed in §6.1.1 (the NFRs prune the topology), as a bounded design specification.

**Argumentative spine (required):**
- **Thesis of this section:** The system is decomposed into a set of loosely-coupled, independently-replaceable modules whose *topology* — not merely whose membership — is determined by the OD-6 non-functional envelope: in particular, PACS/EHR integration is realized as an asynchronous interoperability layer (FHIR/HL7, store-and-forward) that satisfies the interoperability requirement (NFR-6) without placing a hospital-system round trip on the inference critical path, which intermittent connectivity (NFR-4) and no-GPU/sub-16 GB local operation (NFR-1/NFR-2) would otherwise render inadmissible.

  1. Open by taking Tables 6.1–6.2 (from §6.1.1) as the specification to satisfy; restate the design-only status (SB-4.1). Position §6.1.2 as the decomposition obligated by §6.1.1's hand-off.
