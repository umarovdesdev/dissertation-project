**Section objective:** Specify the functional and non-functional requirements of the automated DR screening system as a bounded design specification, deriving the non-functional envelope from the OD-6 resource-limited definition and the functional set from the #22 component decomposition, while fixing the chapter's design-only epistemic status.

- **Thesis of this section:** The system's requirements are determined jointly by the integrated preprocessing-CNN model developed in the preceding chapters (what the system must *do*) and by the OD-6 resource-limited deployment envelope (the conditions under which it must do it); together they yield a closed, design-only requirements specification — not an implemented or validated system.

  1. Open by carrying the Chapter-3 result forward: the integrated model has been *specified as an object of study*; Chapter 6 now treats that same model as a *component to be embedded in a deployable screening system*. State the design-only epistemic status (SB-4.1) up front.

Derive the non-functional requirements (NFR) from OD-6 (no-GPU inference, <16 GB RAM, near-real-time throughput, intermittent connectivity) plus modularity/scalability/interoperability/security from #22 — analytically argue the NFR envelope, not the FR list, is the binding design driver in this deployment context.
