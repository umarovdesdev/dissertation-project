### CONTENT SPECIFICATION

**Section objective:** Specify, as a candidate-original design contribution, the external-image ingestion protocol that conditions heterogeneous clinical fundus images for entry into Stage 0 of the V5 pipeline — format/container normalization, FOV/quality gating, laterality and metadata reconciliation, and label/grade sanity — and bound its validity explicitly to the Kazakh clinical data (NC-15), framing it as a design specification rather than a validated general-purpose cleaner.

**Argumentative spine (required):**
- **Thesis of this section:** The eight-stage pipeline assumes a well-formed fundus image at Stage 0; clinical images do not always satisfy that assumption, so the framework prepends an ingestion protocol that normalizes format, gates on field-of-view recoverability and quality, reconciles laterality and metadata, and validates labels — a methodological contribution whose validation is bounded to the Kazakh clinical dataset (NC-15).
- **Reasoning chain:**
  1. Motivate (from §3.1.3 hand-off): the canonical pipeline assumes a single-fundus RGB image with recoverable FOV; real clinical exports violate this (mixed formats/bit-depths, embedded annotations, ambiguous laterality, missing/out-of-range grades). [candidate-original]
  2. Component 1 — format/container normalization → canonical RGB uint8 input contract for Stage 0.
  3. Component 2 — FOV/quality gating: images whose foreground/FOV cannot be recovered (Stage 2 precondition) are flagged/excluded, not silently processed; ties to OD-1 (image quality operational, downstream).
  4. Component 3 — laterality + metadata reconciliation: supply/repair the laterality signal Stage 0 needs; heuristic fallback when metadata absent.
  5. Component 4 — label/grade sanity: validate 5-class DR 0–4 range; flag missing/invalid.
  6. Boundary: NC-15 (Kazakh-data-bound), SB-2.2 (private, non-reproducible), de-identification → §6.4.1 (SB-4.2). Frame as design contribution; not a validated cleaner (CFC-2.4).
- **Conclusion / hand-off:** With ingestion specified, §3.1 (pipeline formalization) is complete; §3.2 turns to the CNN architectures the pipeline feeds.
