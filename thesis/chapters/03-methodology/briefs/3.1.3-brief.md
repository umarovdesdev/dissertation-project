> **⚠️ SUPERSEDED AUGMENTATION SPEC (updated 2026-06-26).** The Stage-6 chromatic augmentation was changed from **PCA colour jitter** to a **ColorJitter** transform (brightness, contrast, saturation each factor ∈ [0.9, 1.1]; hue shift ∈ [−0.02, 0.02]; each component applied independently with p = 0.5), and two **acquisition-variability** augmentations were added: **Gaussian noise** (σ ∈ [2, 6] on the 8-bit RGB scale, p = 0.15) and **JPEG compression** (quality ∈ [70, 100], p = 0.20). Final on-the-fly order: (1) unified affine; (2) ColorJitter; (3) Gaussian noise; (4) JPEG compression. The current §3.1.3 draft/translation and OD-3 Stage 6 (INVARIANTS v6.1.0) reflect this. The PCA / RES-PCA passages below (eigenvalue analysis, #65 method attribution, DGL-1 basis-binding) are **historical and no longer apply** — RES-PCA is no longer a project artifact and #65 is no longer cited in §3.1.3.

### CONTENT SPECIFICATION

**Section objective:** Specify Stage 6, the train-only augmentation, in full — the unified affine transform (rotation with Stage-1-adaptive dispersion, zoom [0.9, 1.1], optional shear/stretch), brightness/contrast jitter, and the Krizhevsky-style PCA color jitter whose colour basis was actually estimated from the EyePACS training set (RES-PCA) — and explain its dual role: generic overfitting control (regularization, §2.2.3) and, applied with class-conditional emphasis, one lever against EyePACS's severe class imbalance (SC-1.4), with the loss-side lever specified in §3.3.4.

**Argumentative spine (required):**
- **Thesis of this section:** Stage 6 augmentation enlarges and re-weights the effective training distribution through geometry-preserving affine, photometric, and dataset-specific PCA-colour perturbations applied at training time only; it serves simultaneously as regularization and as one of two design levers (with the weighted loss) addressing EyePACS class imbalance, while leaving inference deterministic.
- **Reasoning chain:**
  1. Train-only status and its reason (deterministic inference; augmentation must not leak into validation/test — risk-control, RESEARCH_ARCHITECTURE §9.1).
  2. Unified affine: rotation dispersion adaptive from Stage-1 detection uncertainty (ties to §3.1.1), zoom [0.9, 1.1], shear/stretch; geometry-preserving plausible viewpoint variation.
  3. Brightness/contrast jitter + coordination with Stage-5 stochastic CLAHE (§3.1.2) → contrast-profile robustness.
  4. PCA color jitter (Krizhevsky-style, σ=0.1): add along eigenvectors scaled by eigenvalues × N(0,σ); the basis is the real EyePACS basis (RES-PCA: eigvals ≈ [20.4, 128.1, 2374.0]; one dominant chromatic axis ≈18× the next) — perturbation is predominantly along the dataset's dominant colour-variation direction, a principled colour augmentation rather than arbitrary jitter (cite #65 for the method, RES-PCA for the realized basis).
  5. Dual role: regularization (§2.2.3) + class-imbalance mitigation lever (SC-1.4), the second lever being the weighted loss (§3.3.4); SB-2.1.
- **Conclusion / hand-off:** Stage 6 completes the train-time pipeline; Stage 7 (already specified §3.1.1) finalizes the tensor; §3.1.4 specifies the external-ingestion protocol preceding Stage 0.

### COUNTER-ARGUMENT & INHERITED LIMITATIONS

- **Counter-position:** that augmentation alone can correct severe class imbalance.
