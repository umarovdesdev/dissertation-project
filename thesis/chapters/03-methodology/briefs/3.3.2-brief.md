### CONTENT SPECIFICATION

**Section objective:** Specify the V5-arm initialization — ophthalmology-specific self-supervised pretraining of the same CNN backbone (ResNet-50 / EfficientNet-B3) on an unlabeled retinal fundus corpus, via a CNN-compatible SSL protocol from the DINO/BYOL/SimCLR/MoCo family selected empirically — explain why a CNN-native in-domain initialization (rather than RETFound's ViT-Large or plain ImageNet) is the design that isolates the architecture factor, and bind the whole specification under CFC-2.8 (composite IV) while honestly flagging the literature gap and the untrained status.

**Argumentative spine (required):**
- **Thesis of this section:** In the V5 arm of Experiment 1, the CNN backbone is initialized not from ImageNet but from ophthalmology-specific self-supervised pretraining on an unlabeled retinal fundus corpus, performed on the same backbone used in the baseline arm; this in-domain, CNN-native initialization is the pretraining half of the composite (preprocessing × pretraining) independent variable, so that the H-1 effect is, by construction, attributable only to the integrated configuration and not to preprocessing or pretraining alone (CFC-2.8).
- **Reasoning chain:**
  1. From §3.3.1: adaptation (head) is common; the *initialization* is what distinguishes the arms. Baseline arm = ImageNet; V5 arm = ophthalmology-specific SSL (DGL-6).
  2. SSL protocol: a CNN-compatible domain-adaptive self-supervised protocol from the DINO/BYOL/SimCLR/MoCo family, selected empirically and recorded once chosen; no DR labels used; objective = representation learning over fundus imagery (vascular topology, OD/macular morphology, retinal texture, illumination variability, imaging artifacts) (RESEARCH_ARCHITECTURE §4.2bis).
  3. Why CNN-native and not RETFound (ViT-Large): adopting a ViT foundation model would change both architecture and initialization, confounding the preprocessing contribution with an architecture change; a CNN-native SSL changes only the initialization, preserving the backbone across arms — this resolves AOQ-1→(b), restores the 2×2 symmetry (AOQ-4), and makes the RETFound license moot (AOQ-3) (INVARIANTS v6.0.0 §X).
  4. Input channels: because SSL is performed in-house, the encoder can be pretrained directly on the 4-channel V5 tensor (AOQ-2 simplified); copy-and-mean-init fallback for channel 3.
  5. **CFC-2.8 (central):** the V5 arm differs from baseline along both preprocessing and pretraining; the composite IV is retained; single-factor attribution is forbidden and explicitly relinquished; permissible framing stated verbatim.
  6. **lit-GAP + honesty:** primary in-domain retinal SSL sources (DINO/BYOL/SimCLR/MoCo on fundus) are absent from the corpus (#73 general survey only); the in-domain SSL initialization is a candidate methodological contribution; the SSL arm has not yet been trained — this is a specification, not a result.
- **Conclusion / hand-off:** With the V5-arm initialization specified, §3.3.3 specifies the fine-tuning schedule applied to the adapted, initialized network, and §3.3.4 the loss.
