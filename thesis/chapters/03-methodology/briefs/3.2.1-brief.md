### CONTENT SPECIFICATION

**Section objective:** Specify the two primary CNN backbones — ResNet-50 (residual-connection family, #66) and EfficientNet-B3 (compound-scaling family, #68) — justify the choice of two established architectures from distinct families as the substrate for the H-1 factorial and the EH-4 cross-architecture replication, specify the 3-channel (baseline) vs 4-channel (integrated) input handling and the mixed-precision configuration, and bound the choice (SB-3.1/NC-6: not optimal; SIR-7: B3 ≠ the B0 of prior self-work; CNN-centred by design, DGL-6/§3.3.2 for pretraining).

**Argumentative spine (required):**
- **Thesis of this section:** The CNN component of the model is instantiated by two established backbones from distinct architecture families — ResNet-50 and EfficientNet-B3 — chosen not as claimed-optimal networks but as a matched pair that lets the preprocessing contrast of H-1 be tested for replication across architecture families (EH-4), under a controlled training configuration and a defined 3-vs-4-channel input contract.
- **Reasoning chain:**
  1. From §3.1.4 hand-off: the network consumes the 3-channel (baseline) or 4-channel (integrated) tensor; the architecture is the second model component.
  2. ResNet-50: residual learning (#66), the residual-connection family; depth via identity shortcuts. Dissertation backbone.
  3. EfficientNet-B3: compound scaling of depth/width/resolution (#68), the compound-scaling family. Dissertation backbone. (Context: #69 EfficientNetV2; #70 ViT and #09 hybrids define the CNN-vs-ViT/hybrid landscape — the dissertation is CNN-centred by design, so the integrated-arm SSL is CNN-native, preserving the architecture across arms; ViT not adopted.)
  4. Two families, not one: the replication of any H-1 effect across ResNet-50 (A↔B) and EfficientNet-B3 (C↔D) is the EH-4 cross-architecture requirement; a single architecture could not provide it.
  5. Input-channel handling: baseline 3-channel; 4-channel (RGB+FOV mask) — first-convolution adaptation (copy pretrained weights to channels 0–2, initialize channel 3; AOQ-2 simplified since in-house SSL can pretrain on 4 channels directly, INVARIANTS v6.0.0 §X / RESEARCH_ARCHITECTURE §4.2bis).
  6. Training/hardware config: mixed precision enabled for ResNet-50, disabled for EfficientNet (fp16 overflow) — DGL-2; full config TAB-3.1 (§3.4.2/§4.1.3).
  7. Bound: SB-3.1/NC-6 (not optimal, no exhaustive search); SIR-4/7 (#24 prior 4-layer CNN; B3 ≠ B0); pretraining → §3.3.2 (DGL-6).
- **Conclusion / hand-off:** The two backbones are fixed; §3.2.2 documents the historical reference architectures (OD-2 low/high-complexity), then §3.3 specifies how the backbones are adapted, pretrained, and fine-tuned.
